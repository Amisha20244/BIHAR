Welcome to the Bihar Festival page!

Here you can find information about the various festivals celebrated in Bihar.

Popular Festivals

  • Chhath Puja: A major festival dedicated to the Sun God.
  • Sama Chakeva: A festival celebrating the bond between brothers and sisters.
  • Makar Sankranti: A harvest festival celebrated with kite flying.
  • Bihar Diwas: Celebrated on March 22nd to mark the formation of Bihar as a state.
  • Holi: The festival of colors, celebrated with enthusiasm.
  • Durga Puja: A significant festival dedicated to Goddess Durga.
  • Diwali: The festival of lights, celebrated with joy and fervor.
  • Eid: Celebrated by the Muslim community with prayers and feasting.
  • Christmas: Celebrated by Christians with joy and festivities.
